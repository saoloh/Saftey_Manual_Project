3. Safety Assurance (SA)

Verifying Performance

Safety Assurance verifies that our controls work. We do this by monitoring Safety Performance Indicators (SPIs), conducting Audits, and investigating incidents.

SPI Monitor: Cargo Damage Rate

Target: Less than 5 incidents per 1000 shipments.

Assurance Activity Cycle

SMS Audits Annually

Evaluation of SMS maturity and compliance with ICAO/national regulations.

Output: Executive Review & Corrective Action Plan.

Operational Audits Quarterly

Spot checks of ramp handling, loading, and forklift procedures.

Output: Compliance Score (Target > 85%).

Incident Investigation Event-Driven

Root Cause Analysis (RCA) for all reportable incidents and high-risk near misses.

Output: Action closure within 45 days.

Management of Change Prior to Change

Review of new routes, software, or equipment.

Output: Head of Safety Sign-Off.
